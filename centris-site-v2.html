How it works

AI handles the load.
Humans handle the moment.

A live call moves through five steps. AI does what AI is good at; trained bilingual agents do what humans are good at. Neither replaces the other — they hand off in milliseconds.

01
AI

Listen

Sophia transcribes the call as it happens — EN/ES — and detects tone, intent, and language shift.

02
AI

Analyze

Pull customer history, relevant policy, prior contacts, and sentiment trajectory in under a second.

03
AI

Suggest

Surface the next line, the right offer, the right escalation — with a confidence score.

04
Human

Decide

The agent stays in their voice. Accept the suggestion, rewrite it, or take the call somewhere only a human can.

05
AI

Document

The CRM note writes itself. Sentiment shift, action items, language preference — logged before the call ends.
