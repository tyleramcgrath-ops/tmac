About Centris

For over 30 years,
we've been the team behind the customer service.

Centris Information Services delivers dedicated bilingual contact center teams that operate as a seamless extension of the organizations we support. Our agents — backed by Sophia, our in-call AI assistant — strengthen customer relationships, improve operational efficiency, and help your business grow with confidence.

Our Story

Three decades of contact-center expertise.

From a single nearshore office in the 1990s to a two-hub operation powered by proprietary AI, our story is one of steady investment in people, language, and technology — in that order.

1993

Founded in Longview, Texas

Centris opens with a small team and a simple thesis: U.S. companies need higher-quality bilingual customer service than offshore options can deliver.

2003

Monterrey operations open

Our first nearshore hub launches in Monterrey, MX — same time zones as our U.S. clients, native EN/ES bilingual agents.

2014

Aguascalientes opens · 2 hubs, one network

Second nearshore hub for redundancy and scale. Both sites operate as one shared talent pool with unified training and QA.

2021

AI-driven QA platform launches

We begin scoring every customer conversation in real time — no sampling — with machine-learning-based rubric scoring across all channels.

2024

Sophia goes live in production

Our in-call AI assistant rolls out across all client queues. Live transcription, sentiment, and next-line suggestions — sub-second latency, bilingual by default.

2026

5,000+ partners, 100% scored calls

Centris now supports thousands of customer-facing operations across North America with 100% AI-scored quality coverage.

What we believe

Three principles that shape every customer conversation.

01 — Humans first

AI augments. It never replaces.

Sophia exists to make our agents better — clearer, faster, kinder. The voice on the other end of the call is always human.

02 — Bilingual by default

EN and ES at native quality.

Native fluency isn't a premium tier — it's the floor. Every agent. Every channel. Every customer.

03 — Auditable to the line

Every suggestion, traceable.

Every AI recommendation, every translation, every CRM note — traceable to the call, the policy, the moment.
