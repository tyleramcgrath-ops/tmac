What Tier 1 Tech Support means at Centris.

Tier 1 is where most tech support either succeeds or quietly punts the customer to a ticket queue. We don't punt. Sophia gives the agent live access to your KB and known-issue list, plus context from the customer's prior interactions. The bar is first-contact resolution.

What stays Tier 1, gets resolved. What needs to escalate, gets escalated clean — with full context, repro steps, and the customer briefed.

  • Product-trained agents — dedicated team, not a rotating pool.
  • Live KB & status surfacing — Sophia pulls the right article instantly.
  • Clean escalation — repro steps, logs, and customer context handed off.
  • SLA-tracked — response, resolution, and satisfaction metrics by ticket.
First-contact resolution

If we can solve it without escalating, we will.

Most Tier 1 operations punt anything they don't immediately recognize. We don't. Agents are trained deep on your product, with Sophia surfacing the right KB article and prior ticket history within seconds. Studies show 60-75% of Tier 1 tickets can be closed at first contact when the agent has the right tooling. We hit that bar.

Clean escalation

When it has to go to Tier 2, it goes ready.

The thing engineering teams complain about most is Tier 1 escalations with no repro steps, no logs, and no customer context. We don't do that. Every escalation includes a structured handoff: what the customer reported, what we tried, what we observed, the repro steps, and the customer's expectation. Tier 2 picks up where we left off, not from scratch.
