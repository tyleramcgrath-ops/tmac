FAQ

Questions we get before every kickoff.

Direct answers for teams evaluating nearshore contact center support.

How fast can we go live?

Pilots typically launch in 30–45 days, with full ramp in 60–90 days. We can run a small bilingual pilot in parallel to your existing operation while we tune playbooks and Sophia to your tone and policies.

Will AI replace agents?

No. Sophia listens, drafts, and recommends — the human decides. Every suggestion is auditable to the line, and the agent is always free to accept, rewrite, or skip. AI removes the load; humans handle the moment.

What languages do you cover?

English and Spanish are native across both Mexico centers. We also support real-time EN↔ES translation and live language detection mid-call, with auto-switching for hybrid conversations.

Can you integrate with our CRM?

Yes. Common workflows include Salesforce, Zendesk, HubSpot, ServiceNow, Five9, AWS Connect, and Twilio Flex. CRM notes auto-write at the end of each call, with sentiment, action items, and language preference logged automatically.

How is QA actually done?

100% of interactions are auto-scored against your rubric — no sampling. Coaching dashboards surface trends per agent, per team, per campaign. You get the dashboards. Your supervisors get specific, actionable coaching, not vibes.

Is this HIPAA-compliant?

Yes. We've operated HIPAA-grade healthcare programs for years — Patient Advocacy, RCM, scheduling — across both centers. PHI handling, retention, and access controls are baked into the platform, not bolted on.
