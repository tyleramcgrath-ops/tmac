01 — In action

What human-first AI
actually sounds like.

Centris hears each call as it happens. Sentiment, language, and policy context arrive at the agent before the next breath. Sophia, our in-call AI assistant, suggests what to say — your agents stay in their voice. The CRM note writes itself.

  1. 01
    Listen in real time.

    Live transcription across English and Spanish. Tone, sentiment, and intent extracted as the conversation unfolds — no after-call review.

  2. 02
    Suggest the next line.

    Sophia pulls the right policy, the right offer, the right empathy — surfaced where the agent is already looking. Accept or rewrite.

  3. 03
    Score every call.

    Every conversation, on every channel, against your rubric. No sampling. Coaching becomes specific, not vibes.

  4. 04
    Close the loop in the CRM.

    The call ends — the note is already written, the next step already proposed. Your agents move to the next conversation, not the next form.
