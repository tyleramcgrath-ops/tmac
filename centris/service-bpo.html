What Business Process Outsourcing actually means at Centris.

BPO is where contact center fluency meets back-office discipline. We take on the structured, repeatable work — data entry, document processing, claims intake, order management, light underwriting prep — and run it as a tight, measured operation.

You get throughput, accuracy, and audit trails. We get to do the work we're good at. Your team gets their time back.

  • Document processing — claims, applications, contracts, invoices.
  • Data entry & cleansing — high accuracy, sampled QA, defined SLAs.
  • Order management & fulfillment ops — bilingual where needed.
  • Knowledge process outsourcing — research, validation, light analysis.
